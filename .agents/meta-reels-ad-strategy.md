## Step 2: Ad Creative Strategy

### Format: Vertical Video (9:16)
- **Length:** 8-15 seconds (Reels sweet spot)
- **Style:** Native feel — NOT polished ad. Should feel like organic Reel content
- **Sound:** ON — Reels are sound-on environment
- **Captions:** Always (some watch muted)
- **Hook:** First 1-2 seconds MUST stop the scroll

### 6 Ad Concepts

---

#### Ad 1: "The Grape Test" (Fear → Relief)
**Angle:** Did you know grapes are toxic to dogs? Most owners don't.
**Hook:** Dog sniffing at a grape on a table
**Story:** Danger moment → app saves the day → peace of mind
**CTA:** Download PawBalance — know before you feed

#### Ad 2: "Can My Dog Eat This?" (Curiosity/Relatability)
**Angle:** That moment when your dog stares at your food and you wonder...
**Hook:** Cute dog begging at dinner table
**Story:** Dog begging → owner unsure → opens app → instant answer
**CTA:** Stop guessing. Start knowing.

#### Ad 3: "Home Chef" (Recipe Safety)
**Angle:** Cooking for your dog? Make sure every ingredient is safe.
**Hook:** Someone preparing food in kitchen, dog watching
**Story:** Cooking scene → checks ingredients in app → green safety badges
**CTA:** AI checks your dog's meals. Free to try.

#### Ad 4: "200+ Foods" (Authority)
**Angle:** We checked 200+ foods so you don't have to worry.
**Hook:** Fast montage of foods with Safe/Toxic badges flashing
**Story:** Food montage → app demo → personalized results
**CTA:** Every food. Every breed. One app.

#### Ad 5: "Personalized" (Differentiation)
**Angle:** What's safe for a Lab might not be safe for a Chihuahua.
**Hook:** Two different dogs, same food, different safety ratings
**Story:** Side-by-side comparison → app shows different results per dog → "personalized for YOUR dog"
**CTA:** Your dog is unique. Their nutrition should be too.

#### Ad 6: "The Panic" (Emotional)
**Angle:** That panic when your dog eats something off the floor
**Hook:** Dog eating something quickly, owner rushing over
**Story:** Panic moment → opens app → checks food → relief (it's safe)
**CTA:** Peace of mind in seconds. Download PawBalance.

## Step 5: Post-Production Workflow

### For Each Video:
1. **Generate** video in Seedance 2.0 (paste Chinese prompt)
2. **Download** the raw video
3. **Edit in CapCut** (free, mobile-friendly):
   - Add English text overlays per the overlay instructions above
   - Add "PawBalance" logo at end (use app icon)
   - Add App Store download badge at end
   - Add trending/upbeat background music (CapCut library, royalty-free)
   - Export 9:16, 1080x1920
4. **Upload** to Meta Ads Manager

### Text Overlay Style Guide:
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or SF Pro)
- **Colors:** White text with dark shadow for readability. Red for "toxic/danger." Green for "safe."
- **Position:** Bottom third (avoid center — Instagram UI overlaps)
- **Size:** Large enough to read on mobile
- **App Store badge:** Bottom center, last 2 seconds

## Step 7: Weekly Optimization (After 7 Days)

### Check:
- **CPI** (Cost Per Install) — target under $3 for pet apps
- **CTR** — target >1% for Reels
- **Install rate** — clicks to installs
- **Which ad has lowest CPI** → give it more budget
- **Which ad has highest CPI** → pause after 1000+ impressions if still bad

### Optimization Actions:
1. **Week 1-2:** Don't touch. Let Meta learn.
2. **Week 3:** Pause worst performing ad. Replace with new variation of best performer.
3. **Week 4:** If one ad set clearly wins, shift budget 70/30.
4. **Monthly:** Refresh creative (ad fatigue hits around 2-4 weeks on Reels).

### Budget Scaling (if working):
- $10 → $15 → $20 → $30 (increase 30-50% every 5 days)
- Never more than double in one jump
- Watch CPI — if it spikes after increase, pull back

## What's NOT Needed Yet (Skip for Now)

- Facebook Pixel (app install campaigns don't need it)
- Conversion API (needed later for web events)
- Lookalike audiences (need 1000+ installs first)
- Retargeting (need pixel data first)
- A/B testing framework (budget too small, let Meta optimize)
- Landing page (App Store listing IS the landing page)

## Next Steps After Initial Results

1. **If CPI < $3:** Scale budget gradually
2. **If CPI $3-5:** Test new creative angles, keep budget
3. **If CPI > $5:** Pause, rethink targeting or creative, test new hooks
4. **At 1000+ installs:** Create Lookalike audience from installers → new ad set
5. **At scale:** Add Facebook Feed, Stories, Explore placements
6. **When revenue flows:** Switch optimization from installs to purchase events
